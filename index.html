Monitor Your Herd with DairyBCS App

Body conduction scoring (BCS) is an essential farm management tool. BCS is a technique to assess how thin or fat a cow is on the determined scale. It is the first standalone application on the market to monitor your herd with the application and DairyBCS device.

DairyBCS comes with an industrialized casing. The hardware needs to install on a plane surface. The hardware consists of :

  • DairyBCS RFID antenna id very flexible and can receive tag information from both half duplex (hdx) for full duplex (fdx) tags, placed on ears, neck or leg. this identifies the animals and triggers the video recording.
  • Intel 3d camera captures images of the animals back and hind quarters.
  • DairyBCS data processes renders the images of the animals and sends them to the cloud to be processed. The images are scored, health of the animal defined and the results are available to view via the app. Flags can be set for any animals showing change in bcs or outside of a predetermined threshold.

Keep Track of every herd individually: This application has the record of every cow passed through RFID with its BCS score. Based on Machine Learning algorithms, it notifies the farmer of the critical health status of the cow.

Rate your herd on a global scale system: The application also provides various models to rate your herd based on country-specific or global-specific with a selected breed.

Analysis of the current data with past data: The DairyBSC Application provides a visual representation of the cow BCS score. The previous records can be helpful to solve or monitor current problems.

Cloud is the on-demand availability of computer resources, especially data depository and computing ability, without direct active management by the user.

  • DairyBCS devices send the data to Cloud, and further, the data is processed and stored in the cloud-hosted database.
  • Every 3D point, Cloud will get the correct BCS score with Cloud GPU's help.
  • The Cloud provides secure end-to-end communication between a DairyBCS device and the cloud.
